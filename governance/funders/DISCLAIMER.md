**Non-Certification Notice — Cambium Per-Funder Corpus.**
This corpus is decision-support guidance, not legal advice and not a certification
of compliance. Cambium summarizes and maps publicly stated funder rules to its
internal gates to *assist* a human reviewer; it does not determine, attest, or
guarantee that any application, dataset, or project complies with NIH, NSF, or
any other requirement. **Funder policies change without notice and entries here
may be out of date.** Every rule MUST be re-verified at the funder's official
source (URL and date shown) before you rely on it. A "fresh" or "passing" status
means only that an entry was reviewed within its stated window — it is **not** a
compliance determination. The named human Principal Investigator remains solely
and fully accountable for compliance with all funder and institutional
requirements. When in doubt, consult your institution's Office of Sponsored
Programs / Research Integrity / Compliance office. (Standard: `RESEARCH_CONDUCT.md`;
